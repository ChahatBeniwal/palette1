Monthly Art Challenge

During this boring college life, all of us need that refreshing break that tickles our creativity. Hence, we present, a monthly art challenge!

There will be a new theme every month. Participants will have to create something based on that. Other than this, there is no limitation. The top five entries will feature on our Instagram handle.

Click here for the details for participation

  • One person can submit more than one piece.
  • The theme for each month will be posted here. For this month, it will be available soon.
  • The entries will be mailed to palette@iitgn.ac.in.
  • The participants will have a whole month to send their art pieces.
  • Top five will be posted at the beginning of the next month, with the next month's theme.

July Art Challenge

Welcome to the July Art Challenge! This month's theme is "Drops." Dive into the world of creativity as you explore the beauty, elegance, and mystery behind drops of various forms.

Whether it's raindrops, dewdrops, or teardrops, let your imagination flow. Capture the essence of drops through your preferred medium – be it painting, photography, or digital art.

Share your unique perspective on drops and join our vibrant artistic community. Unleash your creativity, and let your art make a splash!

September Art Challenge

Welcome to the September Art Challenge! This month's theme is "Silhouette." Explore the beauty and drama that comes with capturing the essence of subjects in silhouette.

Experiment with different lighting conditions, angles, and settings to create captivating silhouettes. This challenge encourages you to play with light and shadow, resulting in striking and memorable compositions.

Join our creative community, share your unique interpretations, and let the beauty of silhouettes unfold in your artwork. We look forward to seeing the diverse and artistic ways you bring this theme to life!

October Art Challenge

Welcome to the October Art Challenge! This month's theme is "Eyes." Dive into the captivating world of eyes and explore the depth, emotion, and expression they convey.

Experiment with different styles, colors, and compositions to convey a variety of emotions. Whether you choose to depict realistic eyes or explore abstract and imaginative interpretations, let your creativity shine.

Share your unique perspective on the theme of "Eyes" and join our artistic community in celebrating the expressive power of this fascinating subject. We can't wait to see the diverse and creative ways you interpret this theme!

November Art Challenge

Welcome to the November Art Challenge! This month, we dive into the vibrant and dynamic world of Anime. Immerse yourself in the diverse genres, characters, and storytelling that Anime has to offer.

Whether you're a fan of action-packed shonen, heartwarming slice-of-life, or captivating fantasy, let your creativity shine. Express your love for your favorite anime series or create original characters and scenes inspired by the anime aesthetic.
